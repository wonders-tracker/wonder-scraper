## The Best Way to Track Wonders of the First Prices

WondersTracker is the premier market tracking platform for the Wonders of the First trading card game. Whether you're a collector, investor, or player, we provide the tools you need to make informed decisions.

### What We Track

We aggregate data from multiple marketplaces to give you a complete picture of the market:

- **eBay Sales** - Real-time tracking of completed sales and active listings
- **Blokpax** - Floor prices, sales, and inventory from the official marketplace
- **OpenSea** - NFT sales and collection statistics

### Key Features

#### Real-Time Price Data

Every card page shows live market data including:

- Floor price (average of 4 lowest recent sales)
- Latest sale price and treatment
- Volume-weighted average price (VWAP)
- Active listing count and lowest ask

#### Weekly Market Movers

Every week, we automatically generate a [market movers report](/blog/weekly-movers) showing:

- Top gaining and losing cards
- Volume leaders
- New price highs and lows
- Overall market trends

#### Portfolio Tracking

Track your collection with our portfolio feature:

- Add cards with purchase price and date
- Track profit/loss in real-time
- View performance by treatment or source

### Getting Started

1. **Browse the market** - Visit our [homepage](/) to see all tracked cards
2. **Find a card** - Use search or filters to find specific cards
3. **View details** - Click any card to see full price history and charts
4. **Create an account** - Sign up to track your portfolio
